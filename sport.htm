Boaz and Sport

Boaz had a deep passion for sports. In high school, he was determined to join the Athletes class, even though he was more of a brainy kid than a sporty one. Football one was of his favorite conversation topics, always up to date with the latest news and scores.

This page celebrates the three teams he loved most and highlights one of his favorite activities: acroyoga. He had a tough run with his teams, often supporting those that brought him much frustration. Yet, for better or worse, his unwavering devotion to these teams, despite their many setbacks, was a perfect reflection of his character.

Boaz was a dedicated fan of the New York Knicks. He became hooked on the team in the 90s, although their many playoff upsets have often frustrated him. His favorite player was Allan Houston, the Knicks' shooting guard. He also admired John Starks, the team's point guard, and his determined spirit, despite the many defeats he endured.

As a teenager, Boaz's favorite football teams were English. Alan Shearer was his favorite footballer, and Boaz followed Shearer’s teams with great enthusiasm: Blackburn Rovers and, later, Newcastle United. Just like with the Knicks, Boaz never abandoned both of these teams; he continued to support and follow their games even when they were relegated from the Premier League to the Championship League.

In the last decade of his life, Boaz immersed himself in Acro Yoga. He built his strength and became a vibrant part of the Acro Yoga community, where he discovered both challenges and lasting friendships.
